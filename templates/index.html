💡 Advanced Agentic Operations

🤖 Multi-Agent Orchestration (ADK)

Sequentially routes reported incidents through 7 specialized agents (FraudGuard, Inspector, Safety Officer, Consultant, Coordinator, Evaluator, Analyst) powered by Gemini reasoning.

🔌 Model Context Protocol (MCP)

Decoupled tools for location metadata queries, geospatial duplicate checks, incident registry read/writes, leaderboard sync, and city report compiling.

🛡️ FraudGuard Penalty Layer

Filters duplicate submissions within 150m, validates photo metadata for AI generation or manipulations, and enforces warnings, point deductions, or account locks.

🔎 Reporting Guidelines

📍 Geospatial Integrity

FraudGuard compares your latitude and longitude coordinates with existing registry logs. Submitting reports within 150m of an active hazard triggers duplicate warnings.

📸 Image Authenticity

Gemini verifies uploaded images. Using stock photography or simulated AI-generated graphics triggers trust score penalties or profile restrictions.

🏆 Rewards Scale

Earn reward points based on resolved severity: Low (10 pts), Medium (25 pts), High (50 pts), and Critical Hazards (100 pts). Track your rank in Leaderboards!
